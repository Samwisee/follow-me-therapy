Occupational Therapy

Occupational therapy (OT) is about daily activities, joy, and who we aspire to be.

We help children recover, develop, and maintain the skills for independence and community involvement. Our services are personalized to meet each child’s unique needs.

Occupational therapy can help you to:

  • — Improve fine and gross motor skills
  • — Enhance sensory processing
  • — Develop social skills
  • — Support emotional regulation
  • — Build self-care and independence

Telehealth Services

Telehealth therapy offers a convenient and flexible way to access occupational therapy services from the comfort of your home. By using secure video conferencing, families can connect with their therapist without the need for travel, saving valuable time and making therapy more accessible. This approach not only allows for flexible scheduling to accommodate busy lives but also enables us to reach more families, including those in remote or underserved areas. With telehealth, therapy sessions can be seamlessly integrated into your routine, ensuring consistent and effective support for your child’s development.
